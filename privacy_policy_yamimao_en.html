Privacy Policy — Yamimao

Last updated: 2025-09-06

Yamimao is an entertainment & well-being app providing personalized readings (astrology, numerology, etc.). This page explains what data is processed and for which purposes. For questions: yamimao.app@gmail.com.

1. Data we process

  • Technical data & device identifiers: IP address, device information, and advertising ID (AAID) to display ads (AdMob) and prevent fraud.
  • Usage data: usage events, performance and crash logs (diagnostics) to improve the app.
  • User-provided data: first name/nickname, birth date/time/place and preferences if you enter them to receive personalized readings. Without account creation, this can remain locally on the device. If you subscribe to Yamimao+ and create an account, some data may be synced with our backend (Firebase/Cloud Run) for backup/restore across devices.
  • Payments: for subscriptions, Google Play handles the transaction. We do not store card numbers.

2. Purposes

  • Provide the app features (readings and score).
  • Show ads (free tier) and offer a subscription to remove ads.
  • Measure usage and fix bugs (diagnostics).

3. Sharing with third-parties

We may use third-party services: Google AdMob (ads), Google Play Billing (in-app purchases), Firebase (hosting/sync), analytics/diagnostics providers. These parties may process technical identifiers and usage events under their own policies.

4. Legal bases (EU/EEA)

  • Legitimate interests: app security, fraud prevention, diagnostics.
  • Contract performance: providing the features and the subscription.
  • Consent: personalized advertising and marketing SDKs (UMP consent banner where required).

5. Retention

Technical/diagnostics logs are kept for limited periods. Profile data is retained while your Yamimao+ account is active or until deletion request.

6. Your rights

Under GDPR, you may have rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict, object and port data where applicable. Contact: yamimao.app@gmail.com.

7. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures (e.g., encryption in transit, access controls). No method is 100% secure.

8. Transfers

Some third-party services may process data outside the EU/EEA. We rely on recognized mechanisms (e.g., SCCs) where required.

9. Children

The app is not directed to children under 13.

10. Changes

We may update this policy. The date at the top reflects the latest change.
